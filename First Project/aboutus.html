Our Story

Founded in 1973, our coffee brand has been passionately roasting and brewing exceptional coffee for over five decades. What started as a small family-owned roastery has blossomed into a beloved coffee destination known for its rich aromas and unparalleled taste.

We believe that every cup tells a story — a story of careful selection, expert roasting, and a deep respect for coffee farmers worldwide.

Our Coffee

We source only the finest Arabica beans from sustainable farms across the globe, focusing on quality and ethical practices. Our expert roasters tailor each batch to bring out unique flavor profiles — from fruity and floral to deep, bold, and chocolaty notes.

Our signature roasts balance tradition with innovation, ensuring that every cup delights both the connoisseur and the everyday coffee lover.

Over 50 years of expert roasting and passion in every bean.

Why Choose Us?

We don’t just sell coffee — we create experiences. From farm to cup, our commitment to quality shines through with:

  • Eco-friendly and sustainable practices
  • Small batch roasting for optimal freshness
  • Exceptional customer service and coffee education
